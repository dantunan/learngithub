Beyond the Great Wall and the chain of rugged hills through which it snakes,workers are putting the finishing touches to a clossal ediface. The beam of its roof are curved, with golden tiles remniscient of those that adorn the forbidden city, 70 kilometers to the south east. The building it self curves too, in a shape that its architects say resembles a Ruyi, a traditional Chinese talisman. Its architects say that conveys a longing for fullfilment of the Chinese dream, a cherished slogen of China's leader Xi Jinping, whose dream was China should emerge as a global giant. As the state news agency puts it, the building conveys the "imposing manner of a great power".
